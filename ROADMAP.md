# Roadmap

## Phase 1 — Executive Career Package

Goal: Build a world-class Director-level career package.

Deliverables:

- Career Master
- Resume A — Director Engineering
- Resume B — Senior Engineering Manager
- Resume C — Engineering Manager / Principal Engineer
- LinkedIn Executive Profile
- Interview Playbook

---

## Phase 2 — Career Intelligence MVP

Goal: Convert job search activity into structured intelligence.

Deliverables:

- Job Posting Analyzer
- Resume Match Score
- Keyword Intelligence
- Gap Analysis
- Interview Question Generator

---

## Phase 3 — Career Dashboard

Goal: Track job search performance like an executive operating dashboard.

Deliverables:

- Application Tracker
- Recruiter Tracker
- Interview Tracker
- Company Tracker
- Pipeline Analytics

---

## Phase 4 — AI Career Coach

Goal: Provide daily recommendations for applications, networking, resume tuning, and interview preparation.

Deliverables:

- Daily Recommendations
- Resume Improvement Engine
- Interview Prep Engine
- Recruiter Follow-up Engine
- Salary Negotiation Support

---

## Phase 5 — Career Intelligence SaaS

Goal: Package Career Intelligence as a reusable platform for other engineering leaders.
