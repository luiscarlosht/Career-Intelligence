# Career Intelligence

> An AI-powered platform that helps engineering leaders optimize their careers through resume intelligence, job analysis, interview preparation, and executive career analytics.

---

## Vision

Career Intelligence is the first production plugin of the IntelligenceOS platform.

Its mission is to help software engineering professionals maximize their career opportunities using Artificial Intelligence.

Instead of treating resumes, interviews, networking, and job applications as separate activities, Career Intelligence combines them into a unified intelligence platform.

## Development Principles

- Career Master is the single source of truth.
- Never duplicate career information.
- Every application becomes data.
- Every interview becomes training data.
- Every recruiter interaction becomes learning.
- AI recommendations improve over time.
- Every feature must preserve previous capabilities.
