## Job Summary

Metro Global Ventures LLC is seeking a Director of Engineering to lead a small, established engineering team through its next stage of growth. The company was recently acquired by Metro Global and is positioned to modernize development practices, sharpen execution, and accelerate delivery.

This is a hands-on player-coach role leading approximately twenty engineers across backend, frontend, and QA while staying close enough to code and architecture to make technical decisions.

---

## Responsibilities

### Engineering Leadership & Execution

- Lead and mentor the engineering team across backend, frontend, and QA.
- Own overall technical architecture and make key decisions on system design and technology choices.
- Diagnose and eliminate delivery bottlenecks.
- Establish delivery metrics, sprint cadences, and accountability frameworks.
- Build and scale processes, tools, and practices that enable faster, more reliable releases.

### Product & Roadmap Management

- Partner with leadership to define product vision and translate business objectives into technical roadmaps.
- Own and prioritize the engineering roadmap.
- Balance new features, technical debt, and infrastructure.
- Coordinate backend, frontend, and QA delivery.
- Drive sprint planning, standups, and retrospectives.

### AI Execution

- Define and maintain AI-driven development best practices.
- Evaluate, pilot, and deploy AI tooling into production environments.
- Identify high-value opportunities to embed AI into product and operational workflows.
- Grow into AI leadership with support from the Metro Global portfolio.

---

## Requirements

- 6+ years of software engineering experience.
- 3+ years in engineering leadership.
- Excellent communication skills.
- Ability to translate technical concepts for non-technical stakeholders.
- Experience implementing engineering metrics and KPIs.
- Hands-on technical background with modern web application architectures.
- Java backend development experience.
- Modern frontend framework experience.
- CI/CD, DevOps, and cloud infrastructure experience.
- Comfort working in fast-paced iterative environments.
- Willingness to learn and apply AI in production.
- 1+ year Spring Boot experience.
